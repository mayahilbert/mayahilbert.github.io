Stephanie Rothenberg

In Spring 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic shut down businesses, schools, and community spaces. The New Media Artspace gallery was midway through their spring exhibition when they moved online. In under two weeks, I designed and developed an online viewing room, so the show could go on in a safe, digital space.
